Our Ceramic Coating Application Process

Our ceramic coating service follows a meticulous multi-stage process to ensure optimal bonding and maximum protection. Here's how we transform your vehicle:

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough assessment of your vehicle's paint condition to determine the appropriate prep work and coating options.

2

Wash & Decontamination

We perform a thorough two-bucket wash followed by chemical and physical decontamination to remove embedded contaminants, iron particles, and surface bonded contaminants.

3

Clay Bar Treatment

Special detailing clay removes embedded surface contaminants that washing alone cannot remove, creating a perfectly smooth surface.

4

Paint Correction

We use machine polishing with various pads and compounds to remove swirl marks, scratches, oxidation, and other paint imperfections before coating application.

5

Surface Preparation

Special panel wipes and isopropyl alcohol solutions remove all polishing oils and residues, creating a perfectly clean surface for coating adhesion.

6

Ceramic Coating Application

We meticulously apply the ceramic coating in a controlled environment, ensuring perfect coverage and proper curing of each panel.

7

Inspection & Final Touches

After curing, we perform a detailed inspection and apply final touches to ensure perfect application across the entire vehicle.

Note: Our mobile service brings all necessary equipment to your location. We only require a covered area (garage, carport, etc.) for the coating application to ensure proper dust control and curing conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions About Ceramic Coating

Get answers to common questions about our ceramic coating services in Los Angeles and Orange County.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our ceramic coatings last between 2-5 years depending on the package you choose. Our 2-year coating provides excellent protection at an affordable price point, while our 5-year graphene-infused ceramic coating offers maximum durability and enhanced hydrophobic properties. Proper maintenance can extend the life of any ceramic coating.

How much does ceramic coating cost in Los Angeles?

Our ceramic coating services start at $450 for our 2-Year coating on a standard sedan. Our premium 5-Year Graphene Coating starts at $999. Pricing varies based on vehicle size, condition, and the amount of paint correction needed before coating. We provide exact quotes after inspecting your vehicle.

Is ceramic coating worth the investment?

Yes, ceramic coating is worth the investment for several reasons: 1) It provides years of protection versus months from traditional wax, 2) It maintains your vehicle's appearance and value over time, 3) It makes maintenance easier with its hydrophobic properties, and 4) It provides superior protection against environmental damage. For vehicles you plan to keep for several years, ceramic coating offers excellent value.

How is ceramic coating different from wax?

Unlike traditional waxes that sit on top of your paint and last 2-3 months, ceramic coatings chemically bond with your paint, creating a permanent layer that can only be removed by abrasion. Ceramic coatings offer superior protection, durability (2-5+ years), enhanced hydrophobic properties, and better resistance to chemicals, UV rays, and environmental contaminants.

Do you come to my location to apply ceramic coating?

Yes! As a mobile ceramic coating service in Los Angeles, we bring our professional-grade products and equipment directly to your home or office throughout Los Angeles and Orange County. We require a garage or covered area for the application process to ensure proper curing and dust-free application.

How should I maintain a ceramic coated vehicle?

Maintenance is simple: 1) Wash your vehicle every 2-4 weeks using pH-neutral soap, 2) Avoid automatic car washes with brushes, 3) Apply a ceramic boost spray every 3-4 months (which we can provide), and 4) Schedule an annual inspection to maintain your warranty. We provide detailed care instructions with every coating application.
