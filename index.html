Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mobile detailing services.

Our mobile detailing services take approximately 1-2 hours for Mini Detail, 2-3 hours for Luxury Detail, and 3-5 hours for Max Detail. Ceramic coating applications require 1-2 days depending on vehicle size and condition.

We service all of Los Angeles County and Orange County including Santa Monica, Beverly Hills, Long Beach, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Irvine, and surrounding areas. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside our primary service area.

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that bonds with your vehicle's paint, creating a protective layer that enhances gloss and provides long-lasting protection. Our ceramic coating lasts 2 years, and our graphene coating lasts 5 years with proper maintenance and care.

No, our mobile detailing units are fully self-contained with water tanks, generators, and all equipment necessary to complete the job. We only need access to your vehicle and space to work.

There's minimal preparation needed! Simply remove any personal belongings or valuables you don't want our team to move during the detailing process. For interior detailing, removing excess trash or clutter beforehand can help us deliver better results.
