Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does each service take? +

Service times vary based on package: Jay's Basic takes 1.5-2 hours, Jay's Luxury takes 2.5-3 hours, and Jay's Max takes 4-5 hours. Times may vary slightly based on vehicle size and condition.

Do you service the 90247 area (Gardena)? +

Yes! We have a dedicated service base in Gardena to serve the 90247 area and surrounding South Bay communities. As a local business, we provide priority scheduling for Gardena residents and special discounts for repeat customers in 90247.

What are your service areas? +

We provide free service within 30 miles of both our Buena Park (OC) and Gardena (LA) service bases. This covers most of Los Angeles and Orange County. Beyond 30 miles, we charge $10 for every additional 10 miles.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, Zelle, and Apple Pay. Credit card payments are subject to a 1.75% processing fee.

Do I need to provide water or electricity? +

No, we're completely self-sufficient! We bring our own de-ionized water (which means spot-free results) and 4000W of power generation. All you need to provide is your vehicle and about 10 feet of space around it.
