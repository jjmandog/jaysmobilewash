Why We Use DI Water

Our deionized water system is a game-changer for mobile detailing, delivering superior results every time. Here's why it matters:

Zero Water Spots

Our DI filtration removes all mineral content from water, eliminating the possibility of water spots even in direct sunlight.

The Science:

Water spots occur when mineral deposits are left behind after water evaporates. Our multi-stage deionization system removes 99.9% of all minerals (calcium, magnesium, sodium) from the water through ion exchange resins.

This allows us to perform final rinses in direct sunlight without worrying about spots forming, which is especially important for mobile detailing where we don't always have control over the environment.

Standard tap water in LA contains 150-400 ppm of total dissolved solids. Our DI system reduces this to less than 5 ppm.

Paint Protection

Regular tap water contains mineral deposits that can gradually damage clear coat. Our purified water prevents this long-term etching effect.

How It Works:

Mineral-rich hard water can etch into clear coat over time, especially in hot weather. These minerals (particularly calcium and magnesium) create microscopic damage that dulls the finish and reduces the effectiveness of protective coatings.

By using DI water for our final rinse, we ensure no harmful minerals remain on the surface, preserving your paint's integrity and extending the life of ceramic coatings and sealants.

Over a vehicle's lifetime, repeated exposure to mineral-rich water can cause permanent clear coat damage requiring extensive correction.

Eco-Friendly

Our DI water system requires fewer harsh chemicals and less water overall. It's better for your car and better for the environment.

Environmental Impact:

Our DI water system allows us to use up to 30% less water per detail because pure water is more effective at cleaning without chemical additives. The absence of minerals means less soap is needed for effective cleaning.

Additionally, we can reduce or eliminate the use of harsh drying agents and spot removers that contain chemicals harmful to the environment. Our runoff water contains fewer contaminants.

We save approximately 15-20 gallons of water per vehicle detailed compared to standard wash methods, conserving over 20,000 gallons annually.

Enhanced Shine

Pure water creates a deeper, more brilliant shine on all surfaces, especially glass, chrome, and painted finishes.

Optical Benefits:

The complete absence of minerals in our DI water means no microscopic residues are left behind to diffract light. This results in a purer reflection and greater optical clarity, enhancing the visual impact of your vehicle's finish.

Glass surfaces in particular benefit from DI water cleaning, offering streak-free visibility without any haze. Chrome and polished metals display mirror-like reflections that aren't possible with standard tap water.

Independent testing shows up to 15% improvement in measured reflectivity when vehicles are rinsed with DI water versus tap water.
