Learn More About Ceramic Coating
What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer containing SiO2 (silicon dioxide) that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory paint, creating a permanent protective layer that can only be removed by abrasion.

Key Benefits:
  • Protects against environmental contaminants
  • Makes washing easier - dirt doesn't stick
  • Eliminates the need for waxing
  • Maintains resale value
  • Reduces micro-scratches and swirl marks
Our Process:
  1. Full vehicle decontamination wash
  2. Clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants
  3. Paint correction to remove swirls/scratches
  4. IPA wipe down for optimal bonding
  5. Professional ceramic coating application
  6. Infrared curing for maximum hardness

Learn More About Graphene Technology
What is Graphene Coating?

Graphene is a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal lattice - the strongest material known to science. When infused into ceramic coatings, it creates unprecedented protection that's 200x stronger than steel.

Advanced Benefits:
  • Reduces water spot formation dramatically
  • Flexible coating prevents micro-cracking
  • Superior heat dissipation properties
  • Anti-static dust repelling surface
  • Easier to maintain than ceramic
Why Graphene is Superior:

The honeycomb carbon structure creates an impenetrable barrier while remaining flexible. This prevents the micro-cracking common in traditional ceramics, while the thermal properties eliminate water spot etching - the #1 enemy of ceramic coatings.
