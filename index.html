Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical detailing service take?

Service times vary depending on the package and vehicle size. An exterior wash and wax typically takes 1-2 hours, while a full detail package can take 3-5 hours for optimal results.

Do you need access to water and electricity?

No! Our mobile detailing vans are fully self-contained with water tanks, generators, and all necessary equipment. We don't need access to your utilities.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, cash, and Venmo. Payment is collected after the service is completed and you're satisfied with the results.

Are your products safe for my vehicle?

Yes, we use premium, eco-friendly products that are safe for your vehicle's surfaces and the environment. Our detailing methods are designed to clean effectively while protecting your car's finish.
