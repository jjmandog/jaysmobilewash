Paint Restoration

Expert Paint Correction

Our multi-stage paint correction process removes swirls, scratches, and oxidation to restore your paint to showroom condition. Using professional-grade compounds and equipment for perfect results every time.

Common Paint Defects We Fix

  • â˜…
    Swirl Marks & Micro-Scratches

    Those spider-web patterns visible in direct sunlight caused by improper washing techniques and automatic car washes

  • â˜…
    Oxidation & Fading

    Dull, chalky appearance caused by UV exposure and environmental damage that deteriorates the clear coat

  • â˜…
    Water Spots & Etching

    Hard water deposits and contaminants that physically etch into your clear coat, creating permanent marks

  • â˜…
    Holograms & Buffer Trails

    Uneven surface reflections caused by improper machine polishing from inexperienced detailers

  • â˜…
    Random Isolated Deep Scratches (RIDS)

    Deeper scratches from branches, shopping carts, and other hazards that require specialized treatment

Our Paint Correction Process

  1. Surface Preparation

    Thorough washing, clay bar decontamination, and paint thickness measurement to ensure safe correction

  2. Paint Analysis

    Using specialized lighting to identify all defects and determine the optimal correction approach

  3. Multi-Stage Machine Polishing

    Progressive refinement using diminishing abrasive compounds and various pad combinations

  4. Refinement Stage

    Fine polishing to maximize gloss and clarity with jeweling techniques for mirror-like finish

  5. Surface Preparation for Protection

    Panel wipedown with IPA solution to remove oils and polishing residues

  6. Paint Protection Application

    Sealing corrected paint with ceramic coating, sealant, or wax for long-lasting protection
