Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our mobile detailing service? Find answers below.

How long does each service take?

Our service times vary depending on the package and vehicle size: Jay's Basic usually takes 1.5-2 hours, Jay's Luxury takes 2.5-3 hours, and Jay's Max takes 4-5 hours for a complete transformation.

What areas do you service?

We provide free service within 30 miles of both Buena Park (OC) and Gardena (LA) - our two service bases. We cover all of Los Angeles and Orange County including Torrance, Long Beach, Anaheim, and Irvine. Special focus on the 90247 area and surrounding communities.

What do I need to provide for a mobile detail?

Just access to your vehicle and a legal parking spot. We bring all our own equipment, water, electricity, and supplies. We are fully self-contained and eco-friendly, using minimal water with runoff reclamation systems.

Do you service the 90247 area?

Yes! We provide premium mobile detailing services throughout the 90247 area and all surrounding communities in the South Bay region. Our Gardena base is specifically positioned to serve this area with rapid response times.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule through our website contact form, call us directly at (562) 228-9429, or send us a DM on Instagram @jayswaxandwash. We typically schedule 24-48 hours in advance but can sometimes accommodate same-day service.
