Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a mobile detailing service take?

Service times vary depending on the package you select and the condition of your vehicle. Our Mini Detail typically takes 1-2 hours, Luxury Detail 3-4 hours, and Max Detail 5-6 hours. We'll provide you with a more accurate timeframe when booking based on your specific vehicle.

Do I need to provide water or electricity?

No, our mobile detailing units are fully self-contained with their own water tanks and generators. We bring everything needed to detail your vehicle at your location, making the process completely hassle-free for you.

What areas do you service?

We currently service Los Angeles County, Orange County, and surrounding areas including Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, Pasadena, Long Beach, Irvine, and Newport Beach. You can check if we service your specific location using the ZIP code checker above or by contacting our customer service team.

How often should I get my car detailed?

For optimal results, we recommend detailing your vehicle every 3-4 months. However, this can vary based on your driving conditions, climate, and how often you wash your car. Vehicles that are frequently exposed to harsh elements (sun, salt, pollution) may benefit from more frequent detailing.

What products do you use?

We use professional-grade, eco-friendly detailing products that are safe for your vehicle and the environment. Our ceramic coatings and sealants are from industry-leading manufacturers and provide long-lasting protection. All products are carefully selected to deliver exceptional results without harmful chemicals.
