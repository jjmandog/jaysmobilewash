Our Exterior Detailing Process

Our mobile exterior detailing follows a meticulous multi-step process to ensure every surface is properly cleaned, treated, and protected:

1

Pre-Inspection & Consultation

We examine your vehicle's condition to identify problem areas and customize our approach for your specific needs.

2

Pre-Rinse & Wheel Cleaning

Initial rinse to remove loose dirt followed by specialized wheel and tire cleaning to remove brake dust and road grime.

3

Two-Bucket Hand Wash

Careful hand washing using the two-bucket method with premium microfiber wash mitts to prevent swirls and scratches.

4

Chemical Decontamination

Application of iron remover to dissolve embedded metal particles and other contaminants bonded to the paint surface.

5

Clay Bar Treatment

Physical decontamination using detailing clay to remove remaining embedded particles for a perfectly smooth surface.

6

Paint Polishing (Ultimate Package)

One-step machine polishing to remove light swirls and enhance gloss (included in Ultimate package).

7

Paint Protection Application

Application of premium wax, sealant, or ceramic spray coating to provide lasting protection and enhanced gloss.

8

Trim & Glass Treatment

Exterior trim restoration and glass cleaning with specialized products for a complete finish.

9

Final Inspection

Comprehensive review to ensure every detail meets our quality standards.

Frequently Asked Questions About Exterior Detailing

Get answers to common questions about our exterior car detailing services in Los Angeles and Orange County.

How long does exterior detailing take?

Our Express Exterior takes approximately 1-2 hours, Premium Exterior Detail 3-4 hours, and Ultimate Exterior Detail 5-7 hours. Timing can vary depending on your vehicle's size and condition. We'll provide a specific time estimate when booking.

How does your service differ from a regular car wash?

Unlike automated car washes that use harsh brushes and chemicals, our exterior detailing service uses gentle hand washing techniques, specialized products, and a multi-step process including decontamination, clay bar treatment, and protective sealants. This provides superior cleaning, removes embedded contaminants, and offers lasting protection that regular car washes simply cannot achieve.

How often should I get my car's exterior detailed?

For optimal protection and appearance, we recommend Express Exterior every 2-4 weeks, Premium Exterior Detail every 3-4 months, and Ultimate Exterior Detail with paint correction once or twice a year. Vehicles regularly exposed to harsh conditions (coastal salt air, industrial areas, tree sap) may benefit from more frequent detailing.

Do you need access to water or electricity?

No! Our mobile detailing units are completely self-contained with onboard water tanks and generators. We bring everything needed to deliver professional results, regardless of your location. This makes our service convenient for homes without exterior water access, office parking lots, and apartment complexes.

Can you remove scratches from my car's paint?

Our Ultimate Exterior Detail includes one-step paint correction that can remove light scratches, swirl marks, and minor defects. For deeper scratches or extensive correction, we offer specialized paint correction services that can address more significant imperfections. During our pre-inspection, we'll assess your vehicle's condition and provide honest recommendations.

Are your detailing products safe for all paint types?

Yes, we use professional-grade, pH-balanced products that are safe for all factory paint finishes, including clear coats, matte finishes, and vinyl wraps. Our team is trained to select appropriate products based on your vehicle's specific paint type and condition. We also offer environmentally-friendly options that are effective yet gentle on both your vehicle and the environment.
