The time required depends on the package chosen and the condition of your vehicle. A Mini Detail typically takes 1-2 hours, a Luxury Detail takes 2-3 hours, and a Max Detail can take 3-5 hours. Ceramic coating applications may require 1-2 days depending on the package.

No, our mobile units are fully self-contained. We bring our own water, electricity, and all necessary equipment to your location. All we need is access to your vehicle and enough space to work around it.

For optimal results, we recommend a Mini Detail every 2-4 weeks, a Luxury Detail every 2-3 months, and a Max Detail every 6 months. Ceramic coatings should be maintained according to the manufacturer's recommendations, typically with annual inspections and maintenance.

Our mobile car detailing services in Los Angeles start at $70 for our Jay's Mini Detail package, which includes a hand wash, interior wipe-down and deep vacuuming. Our premium Jay's Luxury package is $130 and our comprehensive Jay's Max package is $200. Pricing may vary slightly based on vehicle size and condition.

Yes, we provide complete mobile detailing services throughout Orange County, including Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Irvine, Anaheim, and surrounding areas. Our fully equipped mobile units bring everything needed to detail your vehicle at your home or office with no extra travel fee within 30 miles of our location.

We accept all major credit cards, digital payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Venmo, Zelle), and cash. Payment is due at the time of service. Please note that a 1.75% processing fee applies to credit card transactions.

If it rains on your scheduled service day, we will contact you to reschedule at no additional charge. For light rain, we can still perform interior detailing services if you have covered parking or a garage available.

Please remove all personal belongings and valuables from your vehicle before your appointment. This helps us clean more effectively and ensures that your items stay safe. If you have specific areas of concern, please point them out to your detailer upon arrival.
