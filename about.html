About Jay's Mobile Wash

The story of Southern California's most trusted mobile detailing service and the passionate team behind it.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Jay Martinez, Jay's Mobile Wash began with a simple vision: to provide exceptional car care without the inconvenience of visiting a physical location.

After years of working at traditional detailing shops, Jay recognized that customers valued their time as much as they valued their vehicles. He invested in a fully-equipped mobile detailing van and began offering services throughout Los Angeles County.

Word quickly spread about the quality and convenience of Jay's services. Within two years, the company expanded to a fleet of custom mobile detailing units serving both Los Angeles and Orange County, with a growing team of certified detailing professionals.

Our Values

Excellence

We're committed to delivering exceptional results with every detail. Our team is trained to meet the highest standards in the industry, using only premium products and equipment.

Respect for Time

We understand that your time is valuable. Our mobile service brings professional detailing to your doorstep, on your schedule, without compromising on quality or results.

Environmental Responsibility

We're dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint. Our DI water system reduces water usage, and we prioritize eco-friendly products whenever possible.

Community

We're proud to be part of the Southern California community. We support local events and charities, and prioritize hiring from within the communities we serve.

Continuous Learning

The detailing industry evolves constantly. We invest in ongoing education and training, ensuring our team is always up-to-date with the latest techniques and products.

Integrity

We believe in transparent pricing, honest recommendations, and standing behind our work. Our reputation is built on trust and delivering on our promises.
