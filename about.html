About Jay's Mobile Wash

The story behind Southern California's premier mobile detailing service

Our Story

Jay's Mobile Wash was founded in 2020 with a simple idea: provide premium car detailing services that come to you. Our founder, Jay, had spent years working at traditional detailing shops and saw firsthand how inconvenient it was for busy professionals to find time to bring their vehicles in for service.

Starting with just one mobile unit and a handful of loyal customers, Jay built the business on a foundation of exceptional service, professional results, and customer convenience. Word spread quickly about the quality of our work, and within two years, we expanded to five mobile units serving all of Los Angeles County and Orange County.

Today, we're proud to be one of Southern California's highest-rated mobile detailing services, with over 500 five-star reviews and a growing team of certified professional detailers.
